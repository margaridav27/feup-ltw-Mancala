Each player has a certain number of holes, by deafult 4, which can be defined at the beginning of the game, and a warehouse, the big hole on the side.

You can only control the holes on your side.

The holes have an initial default value of 4 seeds, except for the warehouse that is empty and is where you store the earned seeds. However, this value is also configurable in the settings area.

In your turn, you take all of the seeds from a small hole of your side and plant them in the following holes, counterclockwise.

If the hole your planting is your warehouse, drop it. If it is the opponent's warehouse, skip it and put it on the holes of your side.

You will play again if the last seed:

  • is planted in your warehouse;
  • is dropped into an empty hole on your side. If so, you store the last seed in the warehouse, as well as the seeds from the opposite hole, the opponent's one.

The game ends when one of the players cannot make another move. The other player stores the leftover seeds in the warehouse.

Wins whoever has more seeds.
